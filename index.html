OCCSC Lucky Draw Tickets — Grand Prize Valued at $10,000

The Ottawa Chinese Community Service Centre (OCCSC) is now selling raffle tickets as part of its annual fundraising campaign. Winners will be announced during the Lucky Draw, which will take place on Tuesday, December 2 at 9pm at the OCCSC’s annual fundraising gala (at the Oriental Chu Shing Restaurant, 691 Somerset St.). The Grand Prize is valued at $10,000 and tickets are going fast. Each ticket is $25 and the prizes include:

1. Grand Prize ($10,000 value): Interlock Driveway Installation by Lionscape Paving Inc.

Interlock laneway and walkway installation by Lionscape Paving Inc. includes 20ft. X 30ft. laneway and 6ft. X 12ft. walkway, totaling 672 square feet of high quality interlock.

2. Second Prize ($432 value): A Pair of Ottawa Senators Tickets + Free Parking

Two tickets to the Ottawa Senators hockey game against the defending Stanley Cup Champions Los Angeles Kings. The game is on Thursday, December 11 at 7:30pm at Canadian Tire Centre in Kanata. Also includes a Free Parking voucher.

3. Third Prize ($200 value): Dreamz KTV Vouchers

Includes four $50 karaoke vouchers for Dreamz KTV in Ottawa.

4. Fourth Prize ($140 value): Saunders Farm Admission Tickets

Includes four $35 admission tickets for Saunders Farm (located near Barrhaven).

5. Fifth Prize ($120 value): Family Fun Pass

Includes one Canadian Aviation & Space Museum family pass; one Agriculture & Food Museum family pass; and two Cosmic Adventures admission vouchers (each voucher allows admission for one adult and one child).

6. Sixth Prize ($80 value): A Pair of Ski Day Passes + Lift Tickets

Includes two Day Passes and two Lift Tickets for the Vorlage Ski Centre in Wakefield, Quebec (just north of Ottawa).

Raffle tickets are $25 and there is no limit on the amount you can buy. The deadline for buying raffle tickets is Tuesday, December 2 at 5pm (however, tickets will also be sold to guests during the fundraising gala). Tickets are available now. Please give generously.

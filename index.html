OCCSC Lucky Draw Tickets — Grand Prize Value $10,000

OCCSC Lucky Draw Tickets

Share Your Moments

The Ottawa Chinese Community Service Centre (OCCSC) is selling lucky draw tickets as part of its annual fundraising campaign from now until December 2nd, with a grand prize valued at $10,000! Tickets are $25 each and there is no limit to the number of chances you can buy!

Winners will be announced at the OCCSC’s annual fundraising gala; any winner not in attendance will be contacted after the event to claim their prize.

1. Grand Prize ($10,000 value): Interlock Driveway Installation by Lionscape Paving Inc.

Interlock laneway and walkway installation by Lionscape Paving Inc. includes 20ft. X 30ft. laneway and 6ft. X 12ft. walkway, totaling 672 square feet of high quality interlock paving.

2. Second Prize ($432 value): A Pair of Ottawa Senators Tickets + Free Parking

Two tickets to see the Ottawa Senators play the defending Stanley Cup Champion Los Angeles Kings at the Canadian Tire Centre in Kanata on Thursday, December 11th at 7:30pm. A Free Parking voucher is included.

3. Third Prize ($200 value): Dreamz KTV Vouchers

Four $50 karaoke vouchers to Dreamz KTV in downtown Ottawa.

4. Fourth Prize ($140 value): Saunders Farm Admission Tickets

Four $35 tickets to Saunders Farm (located near Barrhaven).

5. Fifth Prize ($120 value): Family Fun Passes

Includes one Canadian Aviation & Space Museum family pass; one Agriculture & Food Museum family pass; and two Cosmic Adventures admission vouchers (each voucher allows admission for one adult and one child).

6. Sixth Prize ($80 value): A Pair of Ski Day Passes + Lift Tickets

Two Day Passes and two Lift Tickets for the Vorlage Ski Centre in Wakefield, Quebec (just north of Ottawa).

OCCSC will post the names of the winners in its monthly newsletter and on the ‘Come From China’ website (www.comefromchina.com). All proceeds from the lucky draw tickets will go towards OCCSC's programs and services to help immigrants make a new life in Ottawa.

The deadline for offsite ticket sales is 5pm, December 2nd. However, Gala attendees may purchase tickets until the draw is held later that evening.
